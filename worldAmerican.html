MSC WORLD AMERICA

A New World Of Cruising

Where New Adventures Meet Endless Smiles

Bring the whole family and discover A New World of Cruising on board MSC World America. Create unforgettable memories at Family Aventura, a brand-new multi-level district designed for kids and teens, where the excitement will have them begging to stay. Enjoy newly added attractions like the LEGO®️ Family Game Show, where families compete in timed challenges, and the LEGO®️ Parade, where kids can see their favorite characters in action—only on MSC Cruises. Head to the action-packed Drone Academy, and electrifying bumper cars for extra exhilaration. Looking for even more thrills? Cliffhanger, the only over-water swing ride at sea, is sure to bring on the adrenaline. Look forward to this and more in April 2025 when MSC World America begins sailing from Miami to the Caribbean.

ITINERARIES

Book your MSC World America cruise in the Eastern or Western Caribbean with extended stays at Ocean Cay, our private island marine reserve in The Bahamas, starting in spring 2025.

SHIP HIGHLIGHTS

Where Thrill Seekers Meet The Only Over-Water Swing Ride at Sea

Get ready to experience Cliffhanger, the only over-water swing ride at sea, exclusive to MSC World America. Surrounded by breathtaking views and crafted for those who seek excitement, this swing will take you to new heights. With the wind in your hair and the vast ocean below, we invite you to discover this one-of-a-kind, edge-of-your-seat attraction!

Where Sustainability Meets Technology

Discover a new world of cruising on a ship that is designed for immersive and unique experiences at sea. Powered by Liquified Natural Gas (LNG), MSC World America features the very latest technology to lower its impact on the environment. This innovation extends to its trailblazing design and striking futuristic silhouette. Everything from the ship’s aesthetic to the guest experience on board is next-level.

Where Innovation Meets Beauty

Be captivated by a world of beauty at the World Promenade, where the ocean views and LED light shows are stunning and unmatched. Revel in a world of excitement with award-winning activities for the whole family.

Where Local Cuisine Meets Global Tastes

Enjoy a distinct gastronomic journey of discovery that offers everything from fine dining to casual and informal, with a choice of more than 30 bars, lounges, and dining venues featuring globally-inspired menus.

Where Your Little Ones Meet Their Favorite Place At Sea

Doremiland is a dedicated play area, designed by kids and for kids! On board World America, kids and teens will enjoy the largest kids’ space in the fleet with five complimentary clubs that cater to different age groups from six months to 17 years old and offer extended hours from 9am to midnight: Baby Club Chicco (6-2 years), Miniclub LEGO®️ (3-6 years), Juniors Club LEGO®️ (7-11 years), Young Club (12-14 years), and Teens Club (15-17 years).

From a MasterChef at Sea Juniors’ kids-only cooking competition, to VR experiences and “late-night” parties for teens, Doremiland has activities for all cruisers of all ages. Parents won’t be left out of the fun either! The whole family can shake it off at the new Doremix Family Disco, a first-at-sea LEGO®️ Family Zone and a variety of game shows to choose from.

Where Relaxation Meets Elegance

Be the first to experience the largest MSC Yacht Club on board MSC World America, where elegance and premium service await with luxury suites, exclusive venues, and 24-hour butler and concierge services.

ONBOARD ACTIVITIES

Enjoy awe-inspiring entertainment from morning to night, including three new concert-style shows in the multipurpose Luna Park Arena; five new full-scale theater productions in the World Theatre; four themed experiences in the Panorama Lounge; and surprise, pop-up live exhibits throughout the ship. And as always, the award-winning MSC Cruises family offerings feature state-of-the-art facilities for kids with a dedicated area of the ship as well as a range of kids’ clubs and custom programs designed for each age group.

RESTAURANTS & BUFFETS

MSC World America features 13 dining venues including 6 specialty restaurants such as the classic American-style steakhouse, Butcher’s Cut, the Asian-inspired Kaito Teppanyaki Grill and Sushi Bar, and the Latin American street food-inspired Hola! Tacos & Cantina. MSC World America offers unexpected, interactive experiences with entirely new locations for immersive and unique experiences at sea. Discover 3 ample buffets on board, each offering a distinct ambiance, as well as the ship’s main restaurants.
